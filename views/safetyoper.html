Safety Precautions

Important Safety Tips

  • Wear appropriate safety gear, including eye protection, hearing protection, and gloves.
  • Keep the work area clean and well-lit to avoid accidents.
  • Use push sticks or other tools to guide the material through the saw, keeping your hands away from the blade.
  • Do not reach over or behind the saw blade while it is in motion.
  • Disconnect the power source before performing any maintenance or blade changes.

Additional Safety Precautions

Here are some more safety precautions:

  • Always use a push stick when cutting smaller pieces to keep your hands safely away from the blade.
  • Ensure the blade guard is in place and functioning properly.
  • Do not wear loose clothing or jewelry that could get caught in the saw blade.
  • Keep bystanders, especially children and pets, at a safe distance from the saw.

Follow these safety precautions to ensure safe operation of the Table Saw machine.
